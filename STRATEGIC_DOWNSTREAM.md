# Strategic Downstream Opportunity Framework

## Purpose

The card gives executives a broader view of enterprise value without weakening the defensibility of the core ROI. It uses existing CMS, discovery and scenario data, adds no required input page, and places correlated value below the Facility or Portfolio Value Reconciliation.

## Reconciliation hierarchy

### Base financial case

Includes direct and Paycor-influenced value:

- turnover cost avoidance
- overtime premium reduction
- agency premium reduction
- PBJ administrative efficiency
- confirmed retirable technology spend

These items determine net annual benefit, ROI, benefit-cost ratio and payback.

### Strategic downstream opportunity

Displayed separately and excluded from base ROI:

- census and admissions capacity
- CMS performance context
- SNF VBP financial exposure
- compliance and survey readiness

## Strategic range

The card uses the existing scenario assumption sets:

- low end: conservative scenario
- high end: opportunity scenario

Only modules with an explicit financial scenario are added to the range. CMS performance context is qualitative and is not separately monetized.

## Module logic

### Census & Admissions Capacity

Uses:

- CMS certified beds, when reported
- CMS average residents/day, when reported
- entered or guided-estimate monthly resident value
- existing referral-per-rating and census-protection scenarios
- optional staffing-constrained admissions refinement

The automatic physical-capacity illustration is limited to one through three beds. This is capacity context, not a forecast of admissions.

### CMS Performance Context

Uses:

- overall rating
- staffing rating
- nursing and RN turnover
- agency use
- health deficiencies
- fines
- administrator turnover
- optional protect/improve objective

The module produces a lower, moderate or high protection/readiness priority. It does not predict a future CMS rating and carries no separate dollar amount.

### SNF VBP Financial Exposure

Uses:

`Medicare FFS Part A revenue × 2% withhold exposure × scenario recovery rate`

It remains separate from CMS Five-Star ratings and outside base ROI.

### Compliance & Survey Readiness

Uses:

- PBJ preparation burden
- CMS deficiencies and fines for context
- customer-entered or guided compliance exposure
- optional active-remediation refinement

Only the entered compliance exposure is monetized. The tool does not create an automatic CMS penalty or legal conclusion.

## Optional refinements

The card contains only three optional questions, revealed inside the relevant module:

1. Have staffing constraints caused declined or delayed admissions?
2. Is the stated CMS strategy protection or improvement?
3. Is active compliance or survey remediation underway?

They are not required to calculate the base ROI or display the strategic card.

## Customer-facing language standard

Use:

- may influence
- can support
- scenario-modeled
- correlated opportunity
- outside base ROI
- requires validation

Avoid:

- Paycor will increase the CMS rating
- Paycor will produce admissions
- guaranteed recovery
- automatic compliance avoidance
- VBP stars
